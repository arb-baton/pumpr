Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This policy explains how Pump-r.fun handles information when you use the website, Android app, launchpad, social features, referrals, airdrops, communities, and waitlists.

Information You Provide

We may store profile details, usernames, bios, images, uploaded media, posts, comments, referral names, waitlist emails, support messages, and other content you submit.

Wallet And Auth Data

When you connect a wallet or sign in with a social method, we may store your public wallet address, generated app wallet address, display name, profile image, and related session information. Never share private keys in public posts or support messages.

Blockchain Data

Public blockchain data, including token balances, transactions, token launches, airdrop transfers, and wallet holdings, may be read from Solana, EVM networks, Pump.fun, block explorers, RPC providers, and market APIs.

How We Use Information

We use information to run the app, show profiles and posts, process waitlists and support, calculate rewards, prevent abuse, improve performance, and show launch, referral, community, and airdrop history.

Third Parties

Pump-r uses third-party services such as hosting, databases, wallets, authentication providers, RPC providers, analytics-like operational logs, and blockchain data APIs. Their own policies may also apply.

Your Choices

You can edit profile information in the app where supported. You can disconnect wallets in your wallet app and clear browser or app storage. Public blockchain records cannot be deleted by Pump-r.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the Support page inside Pump-r.fun or contact the official Pump-r social account linked from the app.
